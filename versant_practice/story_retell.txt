Story Retell Prompts (practice summarizing)

1. The city opened a new park near the residential area. The facility increased convenience for
the neighborhood and provided more green space. (Key points: location, purpose, benefits)

2. A small company improved its product and saw growth in the market. The updates addressed
customer feedback and led to higher sales. (Key points: improvements, results, future plans)

3. An event was partially canceled due to bad weather, but organizers offered an alternative
plan and the gathering was still successful. (Key points: problem, response, outcome)

When practicing, listen to or read a short paragraph and then state the main ideas in your own
words aloud. Record yourself to check clarity and completeness.
